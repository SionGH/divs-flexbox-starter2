Hi. I'm Sion.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

I was born and raised in the Canary Islands, lucky enough to have a beach in my city. At twenty-something, I went to London, fell in love with the place and lived there until my thirties. There wasn't a beach but many parks and a river.

In my forties, I fell in love and went to live in South Africa. There there were many beaches and beautiful nature all around. Life wasn't easy there, so I returned to my sunny island again. Here I've been since then.

Today I'm changing careers. Here I'm learning how to flexbox. I have to recognize that becoming a front-end developer isn't easy at all, but it is very interesting and gratifying. I started by becoming a UX/UI designer and went a step further. I'm happy I made this choice.
